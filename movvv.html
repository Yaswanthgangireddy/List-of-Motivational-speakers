Eckhart Tolle

>



Eckhart Tolle is a German-born spiritual teacher and self-help author. His books include The Power of Now: A Guide to Spiritual Enlightenment (1997),and the picture book Guardians of Being (2009). Tolle abandoned his studies after a claimed spiritual awakening and later begin working as a spiritual teacher. He came to prominence as a self-help author beginning in the 2000s, aided through promotion by Oprah Winfrey. His teachings draw from traditions such as Zen Buddhism, Christianity, Sufism, and Hinduism, walthough he remains unaffiliated with any religion.
